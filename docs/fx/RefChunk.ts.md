## RefChunk (interface)

A RefChunk is a RefSubject that is specialized over an Chunk of values.

**Signature**

```ts
export interface RefChunk<in out A, in out E = never, out R = never>
  extends RefSubject.RefSubject<Chunk.Chunk<A>, E, R> {}
```

Added in v1.18.0
